Interior design and landscaping

Botsken Enterprise Limited also specializes in providing excellent interior design & Landscaping services, these include; space planning, designing and project management for both residential and elegant commercial projects in Kenya. We make indoor spaces functional, safe, and beautiful by determining space requirements and selecting essential and decorative items, such as colors, lighting, and materials. We are able to draw, read, and edit blueprints. We also are aware of building codes, inspection regulations, and other considerations, such as accessibility standards.
We typically do the following:
01
Search for and bid on new projects
02
Determine the client’s goals and requirements for the project
03
Consider how the space will be used and how people will move through the space/div>
04
Sketch preliminary design plans, including electrical and partition layouts
05
Specify materials and furnishings, such as lighting, furniture, wall finishes, flooring, and plumbing fixtures
06
Create a timeline for the interior design project and estimate project costs
07
Place orders for materials and oversee the installation of the design elements
08
Oversee construction and coordinate with general building contractors to implement the plans and specifications for the project
09
Visit the site after the project is complete, to ensure that the client is satisfied
We also work closely with architects, civil engineers, mechanical engineers, and construction laborers and helpers to determine how interior spaces will function, look, and be furnished. We read blueprints and are aware of building codes and inspection regulations. We use computer-aided design (CAD) software for most of our drawings. Throughout the design process, we often use building information modeling (BIM) software to create three-dimensional visualizations that include construction elements such as walls or roofs. We specialize in different types of buildings, such as homes, specific rooms, such as bathrooms or kitchens. We also work with home-furnishings stores, providing design services to help customers choose materials and furnishings.
