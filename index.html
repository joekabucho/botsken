OBJECTIVES

The objectives for Botsken Enterprise limited are:

  • Achieve highest level of quality both in product and also service to our esteemed customers.
  • Expand our target export market from Europe to the rest of the globe.
  • Capitalize on the niche market for Transport and logistics & also interior design.

Keys to
Success

Product & Service delivery business in Kenya is a mature industry with very little growth and a very competitive market. Therefore, Botsken Limited will focus on the small niche market of our products & services Exporting our agricultural produce to Europe & selling locally too and also on quality so as to retain its supplier contracts with its chain distributors and local produce sellers. To this end Botsken Enterprise Limited have identified the following key elements to success.

Become “first to market” through use of early harvest of Bixa, Moringa, Cashew Nuts, Sesame seeds & peanuts.

Focus on marketing of Bixa to European distributors.

Aggressively pursue cost analysis and reduction. Capitalize on the company’s close contacts to ensure reliable and consistent product and service delivery.

Concentration on post-harvest care of produce to enhance quality. Market to the high-end quality clientele both locally and globally.
